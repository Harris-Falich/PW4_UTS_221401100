Futaba Sakura (Oracle)

A brilliant hacker and recluse, Futaba Sakura supports the Phantom Thieves as Oracle. Despite her social anxiety, she overcomes challenges to contribute to the team with her technical skills.

Skills:

  • Hacking and technical expertise
  • Supportive skills in battle
  • Emotional growth and resilience

Background:

Futaba's past challenges and her journey with the Phantom Thieves showcase her growth and resilience as a key member of the team.
