Yusuke Kitagawa (Fox)

An artistic and enigmatic swordsman, Yusuke Kitagawa joins the Phantom Thieves as Fox. His Persona, Goemon, reflects his dedication to the arts and his commitment to seeking the truth.

Skills:

  • Artistic swordsmanship
  • Ice and wind skills
  • Quest for truth and beauty

Background:

Yusuke is an artist seeking inspiration and truth. His unique perspective adds depth to the Phantom Thieves.
