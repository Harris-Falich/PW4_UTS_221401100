Makoto Niijima (Queen)

Initially a student council president, Makoto Niijima becomes Queen in the Metaverse. She is known for her intelligence, strategic thinking, and powerful Persona, Johanna.

Skills:

  • Tactical analysis
  • Psychokinetic skills
  • Leadership and determination

Background:

Makoto's sense of justice leads her to the Phantom Thieves. As Queen, she brings her analytical skills and determination to the team.
