Ryuji Sakamoto (Skull)

A former track star with a rebellious spirit, Ryuji Sakamoto joins the Phantom Thieves as Skull. He brings raw power to the team and is known for his fierce determination to stand against injustice.

Skills:

  • Physical strength
  • Electricity skills
  • Rebellious spirit

Background:

Ryuji faced unjust treatment from society, leading him to embrace his rebellious nature and join the Phantom Thieves.
