Haru Okumura (Noir)

Haru Okumura, also known as Noir, is an elegant and kind-hearted heiress. She joins the Phantom Thieves to break free from societal expectations and fights with her powerful Persona, Milady.

Skills:

  • Axe proficiency
  • Psychic and nuclear skills
  • Empathy and determination

Background:

Haru's journey from a sheltered life to joining the Phantom Thieves showcases her determination to shape her own destiny and fight for justice.
